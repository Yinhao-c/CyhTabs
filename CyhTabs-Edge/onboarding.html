Welcome to CyhTabs

Your powerful Edge tab management companion

What is CyhTabs?

CyhTabs is a powerful and elegant Edge extension designed to help you organize and manage your browser tabs efficiently. Save groups of tabs, reopen them with a single click, and keep your browsing experience clean and organized.

Create Tab Groups

Save all your current tabs into organized groups with custom names.

One-Click Open

Reopen all tabs from a saved group instantly with a single click.

Quick Search

Find your saved tab groups instantly with the built-in search functionality.

Dark & Light Modes

Switch between dark and light themes to match your preference.

How to Use CyhTabs

1

Click the Extension Icon

Click the CyhTabs icon in your Edge toolbar to open the extension popup.

2

Create a New Group

Click the "New" button, give your group a name, and select which tabs to include.

3

Save Your Group

Click "Save Group" to save your tab collection. You can create as many groups as you need.

4

Manage Your Groups

Open, rename, or delete your saved groups anytime. Use the search bar to find groups quickly.
